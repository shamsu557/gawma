Committed to a Cleaner Kano

Ensuring a sustainable and healthy environment for all residents.

Welcome To Galaxy Waste Management Agency (GAWMA)

Galaxy Waste Management Agency (GAWMA) is responsible for ensuring a clean, healthy, and sustainable environment in Kano State. Established to address the growing challenges of waste management in Kano and surrounding areas, GAWMA is committed to modern, efficient, and environmentally friendly waste disposal practices. Recognizing the rapid urbanization and increasing population, GAWMA was created to provide an organized and professional approach to waste management. Our mission is to promote public health, prevent environmental hazards, and ensure proper waste disposal through strategic planning and innovative solutions. We work in collaboration with local authorities, private waste management firms, and the community to enhance sanitation and keep our environment clean. Our services range from solid waste collection and recycling initiatives to public awareness campaigns and educational programs aimed at fostering a culture of environmental responsibility.

GAWMA View

GAWMA envisions a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable environment for all. Our mission is to revolutionize waste management through innovation, public engagement, and efficient waste disposal strategies. We are committed to fostering a waste-free Kano, where communities thrive in a pollution-free and eco-friendly atmosphere.

What We Do

Disposal Sites

Solid waste collected from all over the state is transported to and received at our disposal sites. These sites are managed efficiently to ensure minimal environmental impact and compliance with safety regulations.

Education & Awareness

GAWMA actively engages the community through awareness programs and educational campaigns. We conduct training sessions, workshops, and outreach initiatives to foster environmental responsibility.

Achievements & Awards

Recognized as the most enterprising governmental agency by the Federal Ministry of Commerce, GAWMA has received accolades for its innovative approach to waste management and sustainability.

Resource Recovery & Recycling

GAWMA promotes recycling and resource recovery to reduce waste pollution. Our programs focus on converting organic waste into compost, reusing plastics, and developing sustainable waste management practices.

Operations

Our operations are decentralized into three major districts: Western, Eastern, and Central. This structure ensures efficient waste collection, quick response times, and localized waste management solutions.

GAWMA Academy

GAWMA Academy provides training on sustainable waste management for professionals and the public. Our courses cover recycling, waste disposal techniques, and environmental conservation strategies.

News & Feeds

GAWMA Launches Clean Kano Campaign

Date: March 10, 2025

To enhance community sanitation, GAWMA has launched a cleanup initiative encouraging residents to take responsibility for their environment. This initiative includes street cleaning, waste segregation training, and household waste management guidance.

GAWMA Introduces Smart Waste Bins

Date: February 25, 2025

In a bid to improve waste collection efficiency, GAWMA is rolling out smart waste bins across strategic locations. These bins are designed with sensors to monitor waste levels and alert collection teams for timely disposal.

Environmental Awareness Program in Schools

Date: February 15, 2025

GAWMA, in partnership with local schools, has launched an educational program to teach students about waste management and environmental conservation. The initiative includes workshops, competitions, and field activities.

New Waste Collection Vehicles Deployed

Date: January 30, 2025

To boost waste collection services, GAWMA has added 20 new waste collection trucks to its fleet, ensuring faster and more efficient waste disposal across Kano.

GAWMA Partners with Recycling Firms

Date: January 15, 2025

In an effort to promote recycling and reduce landfill waste, GAWMA has signed agreements with multiple recycling firms to process plastics, paper, and organic waste efficiently.

Monthly Community Clean-Up Drive Launched

Date: January 5, 2025

GAWMA has introduced a monthly clean-up drive, engaging volunteers and local businesses in keeping Kano's streets clean. The program is aimed at fostering community participation in waste management.
